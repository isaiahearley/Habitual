Welcome to Habitual, a minimal productivity application designed to help you stay organized and build lasting habits without the distractions and clutter often found in traditional apps. I created this project out of a personal need to simplify my workflow and stay consistent. Many productivity tools, such as Notion or advanced calendars, can feel overwhelming with too many features that take more time to manage than the tasks themselves. Habitual is built with simplicity at its core allowing you to quickly log tasks, assign them to a day or time, and focus on completing them, rather than spending hours learning how to use it. Unlike complex scheduling tools, Habitual supports both structured and spontaneous productivity styles. Whether you like to plan weeks ahead or prefer to add tasks on the spot, the app adapts to your habits. The inspiration also came from the idea that productivity isn’t just about checking boxes, it’s about forming routines in the simplest way possible.
